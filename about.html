About Detained

Detained is more than just a band—it's an experience. With a distinct identity shaped by bold visuals and an immersive sound, the group thrives on energy, emotion, and raw musicality. Their aesthetic, defined by sharp triangular themes and a striking contrast of white and deep violet against black, mirrors their edgy yet refined style. While they haven't released originals yet, their journey is one of constant evolution, pushing creative boundaries with each performance. Fueled by passion and a relentless drive to make their mark, Detained is set to carve its own path in the world of music.
